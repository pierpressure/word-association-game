Word Association Game

How to Play

  • You have 3 guesses to find words related to a hidden target word
  • Each guess gets a score based on how semantically similar it is
  • The closer your word is to the target, the more points you earn!
  • Perfect Match (100%): 1000 points
  • Very Close (90%+): 200 points
  • Close (70-89%): 100 points
  • Getting There (50-69%): 50 points
  • Trying (30-49%): 20 points
  • Earlier guesses earn more points
  • Win bonus for remaining guesses
  • Streak bonus for consecutive good guesses
  • First hint is free, additional hints cost points
